About Oscar

Since I was a child I've always loved technology. I've always loved knowing how things operated or what made them work. As I got older, smartphones and personal computers were something that everyone had and I've always had this constant dream that someone will be using an app that makes their life easier, an application I myself composed and put together. That is why I joined The Iron Yard.

The three months that I spent at The Iron Yard were nothing short of amazing. Everyday my will and brain power were tested to the best of their abilities. Graduating from The Iron Yard has defintely changed the way I think but it has also given me the ability to write awesome code.
